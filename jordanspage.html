About Me

I am from Cedar Rapids, Iowa, and I am majoring in Electrical Engineering at the University of Iowa with an interest in analog circuit design.

Experience

Over the summer of 2023, I had the opportunity to intern for Schneider Electric and work on circuit breakers. Specifically, I was part of the team that worked on the trip solenoid and calculating inductances and forces that are generated depending on the material. These would then be tested in a lab with different temperatures to make sure there is enough force to trip the breaker.
